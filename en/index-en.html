Prototype Cabin

Prototype

Use

With the construction of the prototype p-01 we essentially combine three points, which among other things also represent important driving forces for us.

Creating your own product is an already over several generations in which the carpentry was built, pursued dream. This finds now its realization in that very one development and refers to the collected experience ​​of our own camping experiences on.

In addition, it is not just about the short-term technical feasibility under laboratory conditions to check but also the longevity to prove. For this purpose a close exchange with technical experts maintained in order to continuous reviews and adjustments to be incorporated into the prototype. The materials are partly in a brought new application environment, albeit they have been tried and tested for many years.

The acceptance of the market represents another (possibly the decisive) criterion regarding the design of the following salable products. With the prototype the thesis should be checked to what extent a demand at a cube model consists.

Dimensions

Weights

The cabin is designed for three adults. There is an approx. 2.00m x 1.60m bed in the alcove and one 2.00m x 0.90m pull-out sleeping area available. Overall, the cabin has a body length of 4160mm (corresponds to the total length of the cub e ) and 2090mm total width. The headroom in the Interior space will be around 1950mm. The empty weight of the the cabin is in the range of 700 - 750 kilograms. The prototype cabin is designed for a Nissan Navara, however, due to the modular substructure concept can be used on all known Double Cab models. Adjustments to larger loading areas are also conceivable.

Materials

Components

We attach great importance to the selection of materials on the quality, weight and sustainability. Starting with the exterior cladding, we use here no fiberglass composite materials (GRP), but set one used in facade and boat building Composite one that has very similar properties to Has wood, so it is also paintable and malleable. The material consists of more than 60% renewable Raw materials and can be recycled can be reused.

The p-01 is with a bathroom including shower cubicle and separating toilet equipped. Here is the material the outer shell used as wall protection to to take up the character of the outer shell and to use their water-repellent properties. There is a 2-zone induction field for cooking provided that has two photovoltaic panels or operated from a lithium-ion battery. The interior is illuminated by LEDs. There are adjustable reading lamps in the bed area intended. The big windows are supposed to give the room moreover a natural one Give brightness, this is reinforced by Wood paneling in the ceiling and wall area.

Design

Functionality

The outer form and material is a specialty design that has not yet been found on the market. On the one hand, the elliptical alcove contributes to the special external appearance, on the other hand it contributes to lesser flow resistance (compared to cabins other manufacturers) and reduced driving noises.

The combination of attractive design and high functionality is particularly important in small spaces are crucial for the comfortable character and usability. In this regard, a minimalist approach is taken in the choice of furnishing materials and aimed for textures. The interior is characterized by large window elements, as well as the entrance door, which can be folded on both sides. On This is a particular highlight when it is not in use the bathroom element is closed to a small size of only 450mm of the cabin width. This can be doubled in use, which creates a size ratio that is only available in much larger camping structures can be found.

For the final determination of the interior and exterior fittings the p-01 over several weeks from different people tested and optimizations carried out. Here will then again the modular approach of high customizability be juxtaposed.

About us

Who are we? The way to the cabin in brief.

  • We have been involved in interior design since the 1950s. The company was named by Theodor Walk as a one man company, founded in 1949 with only a few machines.
  • The company grew over the years and made its way through theirs high-quality work a name in private interior construction, as well as with large contract facilities. Through time ideas grew to develop, build and sell own products.
  • Then it was during a hot summer day in 2019 the decisive moment came when Heike and Michael Walk spotted a interesting camping vehicle on their Corsica vacation with a caravan. This later turned out to be a pickup including a cabin attachment. Due to their own experiences with campervans and caranvs they decided to built their very own campervan. After visiting several trade fairs and research within the same year the conclusion was clear: a cabin with a pickup would bring the perfect balance between vacation and demands of the everyday life. The realization process of add-on cabin was initialzed.
